Why Fracture

One festival. Zero straight lines.

A real grid, gleefully torn up. Here's what holds the chaos together.

1

Nine stages

From the techno bunker to the open-air type-and-screenprint yard, every warehouse runs its own programme.

2

Print & art

Live riso, letterpress and zine-making between sets. Walk out with art still wet on the page.

3

Until late

Doors at noon, last act at 5am. Camp on-site or stumble home along the harbour.
